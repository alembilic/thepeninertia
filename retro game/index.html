NATURAL SELECTION

ABOUT THE GAME


Have you got what it takes to save your species? Have you got the survival skills to make sure you don't go extinct? If you've answered yes to both questions then welcome to NATURAL SELECTION. A game where you'll be pushed to your limits and you must eliminate your opponents to save yourself and your species. Don't be like the dodo bird... SURVIVE

FEATURES





The Game


Natural Selection is a fast paced, 2D pixel graphic game which operates on a turn based function. It has an online multiplayer mode, allowing you to challenge your friends to reveal who truly is the most strategic.




Armory


Players will be able to choose from a range of weapons that will allow them to damage their opponents. The weapons will have specific amounts of ammo with some weapons doing more damage than others.




Strategy


Players will have a time limit to perform their desired actions and the weapons have limited use.Think carefully before deciding to attack!




Online


The game will support up to 4 player online.Challenge your friends and see who comes up victorious.




COMMANDS



UP / W = JUMP


LEFT / A = WALK LEFT


RIGHT / D = WALK RIGHT
